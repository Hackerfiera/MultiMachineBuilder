NOTICE ABOUT DEPICTIONS OF ALCOHOL

The alcohol is depicted as realistically as possible using avaliable implementations.
Ingame effects:
0 drinks - sober
1..2 drinks - faster blinking, small jitter
2..5 drinks - moderate jitter, slower blinking
5..10 drinks - heavy jitter
10..13 drinks - loss of control, very heavy jitter
13..15 drinks - quick loss of health and death
