Types of road tracks:

Railway - rail vehicles are bound to this type of lane
Lane - road vehicles can enter and exit
Buffer - painted, no traffic allowed
Curb - similar behavior to slab blocks (logic circuitry/pavement)
Beam - similar behavior to scaffolds
Concrete - similar behavior to wall blocks

Parameters of each lane:
leftEntry - allow entry from left
rightEntry - allow entry from right
type - type of lane
vehicles - types of traffic allowed
